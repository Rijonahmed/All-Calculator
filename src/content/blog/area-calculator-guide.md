## Why Use an Area Calculator?

Calculating room and house area is essential for:

- **Real Estate**: Determining property value and listing square footage
- **Flooring**: Estimating how much tile, carpet, or hardwood you need
- **Painting**: Calculating wall area for paint coverage
- **Furniture**: Planning room layouts
- **HVAC**: Sizing air conditioning units

Our [**Area Calculator**](/area-calculator) handles single rooms or multiple rooms to give you a complete house total.

## How to Use the Area Calculator

1. **Add a Room**: Enter a room name (e.g., "Living Room")
2. **Enter Dimensions**: Input length and width
3. **Select Unit**: Choose Feet, Inches, or Meters
4. **Add More Rooms**: Click "Add Another Room" for multi-room calculations
5. **View Total**: The sidebar shows the combined area in both sq ft and m²

## Calculation Methods

### Square/Rectangle Rooms
- Area = Length × Width
- This is the most common room shape

### L-Shaped Rooms
For L-shaped rooms, divide the room into two rectangles:
- Calculate each rectangle's area separately
- Add them together

### Irregular Rooms
- Break the room into smaller geometric shapes
- Calculate each shape's area
- Sum the total

## Unit Conversions

Our calculator handles:

| From | To | Formula |
|------|----|---------|
| Sq Feet | Sq Meters | Multiply by 0.0929 |
| Sq Meters | Sq Feet | Multiply by 10.764 |
| Sq Inches | Sq Feet | Divide by 144 |

## Tips for Accurate Measurements

- Use a laser measure for the most accurate readings
- For large rooms, measure twice to be sure
- Include alcoves and bay windows in your measurements
- For flooring, add 10% extra for cuts and waste
- When measuring for paint, consider wall height (we'll add a paint calculator soon!)
