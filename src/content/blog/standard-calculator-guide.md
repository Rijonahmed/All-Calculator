## Why Our Standard Calculator?

Our [Standard Calculator](/standard-calculator) provides a clean, intuitive interface for quick arithmetic. Whether you're calculating a restaurant tip, balancing a budget, or helping your kids with homework, our tool has you covered.

## Basic Operations

Our calculator supports all the standard arithmetic operations:

- **Addition (+)**: Sum two or more numbers
- **Subtraction (−)**: Find the difference between numbers
- **Multiplication (×)**: Multiply numbers together
- **Division (÷)**: Divide one number by another

## Keyboard Shortcuts

For power users, our calculator supports keyboard input:

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| 0-9 | Number input |
| +, -, *, / | Operators |
| Enter or = | Calculate result |
| Escape or C | Clear all |
| Backspace | Delete last digit |
| . (period) | Decimal point |

## Useful Everyday Calculations

Here are some practical ways to use the standard calculator:

### Tip Calculator
Multiply your bill amount by 0.15 (for 15%) or 0.20 (for 20%).

### Percentage Discounts
Multiply the original price by (1 - discount%). For example, 30% off $80: 80 × 0.70 = $56.

### Unit Price Comparison
Divide the total price by the quantity to find the price per unit.

### Square Footage
Multiply length × width for area calculations (though our Area Calculator is even better for this).

## Tips for Accuracy

- Double-check your inputs before hitting equals
- Use the C button to start fresh if you make a mistake
- For complex multi-step calculations, write down intermediate results
