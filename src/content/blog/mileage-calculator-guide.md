## What is Fuel Mileage?

Fuel mileage (or fuel efficiency) measures how far your vehicle can travel on a specific amount of fuel. It's a crucial metric for:

- Understanding your vehicle's running costs
- Detecting potential engine problems
- Planning road trips and fuel budgets
- Comparing vehicles before purchase

## Understanding the Numbers

### Good Fuel Efficiency (Cars)
| Vehicle Type | Good km/L | Good MPG |
|-------------|-----------|----------|
| Compact Car | 15-20 | 35-47 |
| Sedan | 12-17 | 28-40 |

## Tips to Improve Fuel Efficiency

1. **Maintain proper tire pressure** — underinflated tires increase fuel consumption
2. **Drive smoothly** — avoid aggressive acceleration and braking
3. **Remove excess weight** — extra cargo reduces mileage
4. **Use cruise control** on highways
5. **Keep your engine tuned** — regular maintenance helps
6. **Reduce idling** — turn off the engine if stopped for more than a minute
