## Overview of Inheritance Laws

### Bangladesh — Muslim Law
Under Islamic inheritance law:
- Fixed shares are allocated to specific heirs (Quranic heirs)
- Males typically receive double the share of females in the same class
- The residue goes to male agnatic relatives

### India — Hindu Succession Act
Under the Hindu Succession Act:
- Class I heirs inherit equally (mother, spouse, children)
- Daughters have the same rights as sons
- Inheritance is per capita (equal shares)

### USA — Intestate Succession
- If there's a surviving spouse + children: spouse usually gets 1/3 to 1/2
- If no spouse, children inherit equally
- If no children, parents inherit
- Laws vary by state
