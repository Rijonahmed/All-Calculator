## What is an Age Calculator?

An age calculator is a simple yet powerful tool that computes your exact age based on your date of birth and a reference date. Unlike a basic year-difference calculation, a good age calculator breaks down your age into years, months, days, hours, and even minutes.

Our [**Age Calculator**](/age-calculator) is designed to be precise, accounting for leap years, month lengths, and even leap day birthdays (February 29).

## How to Use Our Age Calculator

Using the calculator is straightforward:

1. **Enter Your Date of Birth**: Select your birth month, day, and year from the dropdown menus.
2. **Choose a Reference Date**: By default, the calculator uses today's date. But you can select any past or future date to see how old you were (or will be) on that specific day.
3. **Click Calculate**: The tool instantly computes your exact age breakdown.

## What Results You'll Get

After hitting calculate, you'll see:

- **Years, Months, and Days**: The classic age breakdown
- **Total Months**: How many months you've been alive
- **Total Weeks**: Your age in weeks
- **Total Days**: The exact number of days since birth
- **Total Hours and Minutes**: For those who want extreme precision

## Special Feature: Leap Day Birthdays

Born on **February 29**? The calculator handles that too! It will tell you how many actual February 29 birthdays you've had throughout your life.

## Tips for Accurate Results

- Make sure your date of birth is correct (especially the year)
- If calculating for a specific date, verify the reference date is correct
- The calculator accounts for different month lengths automatically

## When to Use an Age Calculator

- Checking eligibility for age-restricted services
- Planning birthday celebrations
- Calculating retirement dates
- Filling out official forms requiring exact age
- Just for fun — to see your age down to the minute!
