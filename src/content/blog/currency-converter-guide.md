## What is Currency Conversion?

Currency conversion is the process of exchanging one country's currency for another at the current market rate. Exchange rates fluctuate constantly based on global economic factors.

## Understanding Exchange Rates

Exchange rates tell you how much one currency is worth in terms of another. For example, if USD/BDT is 110, it means 1 US Dollar equals 110 Bangladeshi Taka.

**Factors affecting exchange rates:**
- Interest rates
- Inflation
- Political stability
- Economic performance
- Market speculation

## Tips for Better Conversions

- Compare rates across multiple sources for large amounts
- Be aware that banks and money changers may add fees
- Rates are for informational purposes — actual rates may vary slightly
- For travel, it's often better to use local ATMs than airport exchange counters
