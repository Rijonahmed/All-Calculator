## The Real Cost of a Road Trip

A road trip is one of the best ways to travel — freedom, flexibility, and adventure. But fuel costs can add up quickly if you don't plan ahead.

Let's break down exactly how to calculate your trip's fuel cost using our [Mileage Calculator](/mileage-calculator).

## Step 1: Know Your Distance

First, determine your total travel distance:

- Use Google Maps or Waze to find the route distance
- Add extra mileage for detours, sightseeing, and unplanned stops
- For round trips, double the one-way distance

**Example:** A 500 km one-way trip is actually 1,000 km round trip.

## Step 2: Know Your Vehicle's Mileage

Use the [Mileage Calculator](/mileage-calculator) to determine your actual fuel efficiency:

1. Fill your tank completely and reset your trip meter
2. Drive normally until tank is near empty
3. Fill up again and note the liters/gallons added
4. Use the calculator: Distance ÷ Fuel Used = km/L or MPG

### Typical Mileage Reference

| Vehicle | City (km/L) | Highway (km/L) |
|---------|-------------|----------------|
| Compact car | 12-15 | 18-22 |
| Sedan | 10-14 | 16-20 |
| SUV | 6-10 | 12-16 |
| Motorcycle | 35-50 | 50-70 |

## Step 3: Calculate Total Fuel Needed

The formula is simple:

**Total Fuel = Total Distance ÷ Mileage**

For a 1,000 km trip in a car getting 15 km/L:

> 1,000 ÷ 15 = **~67 liters of fuel needed**

## Step 4: Calculate the Cost

Multiply the total fuel by your local fuel price:

**Total Cost = Total Fuel × Fuel Price per Liter**

With fuel at 110 BDT/liter:

> 67 × 110 = **7,370 BDT for the trip**

## Tips to Reduce Road Trip Fuel Costs

1. **Drive at steady speeds** — 80-90 km/h is typically most efficient
2. **Use cruise control** on highways
3. **Check tire pressure** — underinflated tires can reduce mileage by 3-5%
4. **Avoid excessive idling** — turn off the engine if stopped > 1 minute
5. **Remove roof racks** when not in use — they create drag
6. **Travel light** — extra weight burns extra fuel
7. **Plan your route** — avoid traffic and road construction

## Beyond Fuel — Other Road Trip Costs

Don't forget these expenses:

- **Tolls and highway fees**
- **Accommodation** if multi-day trip
- **Food and drinks**
- **Emergency fund** for breakdowns
- **Vehicle maintenance** (oil change before long trips)

## Quick Budget Example

A 3-day, 1,000 km road trip for two people:

| Expense | Estimated Cost |
|---------|---------------|
| Fuel (67L @ 110/liter) | 7,370 BDT |
| Tolls | 500-1,000 BDT |
| Accommodation (2 nights) | 4,000-6,000 BDT |
| Food (3 days × 2) | 3,000-4,000 BDT |
| **Total** | **~15,000-18,000 BDT** |
